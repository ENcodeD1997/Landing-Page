Why Pets Are Important To Humans

8th January 2023 by Javonte Terrell Bolton

For thousands of years, humans have been resourceful at making nothing out of something.
Luckily we weren't alone in our efforts. We have roamed the earth evolving and improving
our ways of life day by day; luckily, we weren't alone in our effortless journey! Growing and
changing, we were able to start sharing our lives with some things that aren't quite human.

Some history on domesticated animals

Pets have been living alongside humans for possibly what could be considered thousands,
if not tens of thousands of years. Dogs were some of the first animals that humans domesticated.
The exact time frame has yet to be discovered. However, the period is speculated to be between 13,000
and 30,000 thousand years ago across Asia, Europe, and Australia, suggesting that we have
had our four-legged companions for a long time
